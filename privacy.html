Privacy Policy

Updater Bot respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

  • Support Server Logs: Updater Bot may store logs of server activities only within the official Updater Bot Support Server. This includes deleted or edited messages, moderation actions, nickname changes, role changes, joins/leaves, and channel updates. These logs are used strictly for moderation, troubleshooting, and server management purposes.
  • Bot Functionality Data: To operate correctly in your server, Updater Bot collects certain configuration data, including server IDs, channel IDs, and command-specific settings. This data is stored securely in our database (MongoDB) and is used solely for bot functionality. It is not used to monitor messages or other activity outside the Support Server.

3. Data Sharing

4. Data Retention

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system can be 100% secure, and you use the bot at your own risk.

6. Changes to Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time without prior notice. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
